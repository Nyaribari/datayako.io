About Us

Datayako is a data company that uses innovative approaches to give solutions to companies concerning their data.We use a combination of text mining, statistics, network analysis and other approaches to make the invisible and the neglected visible.

We have a strong commitment to working with the music industry, growing businesses, startups and teams to address their knowledge and capacity needs that will contribute to growth, development and enhance innovation. This commitment gets us out of the bed in the morning and is the animating principle behind what we do.
